Artificial Intelligence

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans.

AI Components

  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics

Applications of AI

AI is used in various fields, including:

  • Healthcare
  • Finance
  • Education
  • Autonomous Vehicles

Future of AI

The future of AI holds great potential for advancements in technology, automation, and problem-solving across different industries.
